Paper Submission Guidelines

  • All papers must be submitted in English.
  • Papers must be original and not submitted or published elsewhere.
  • Length: 6–10 pages (IOS Press FAIA/ATDE format)
  • Format: IOS Press Conference Proceedings Template (Word or LaTeX)
  • Submission Link: [EasyChair or ConfTool – to be provided]
  • Peer Review: Double-blind, minimum 2 reviewers per paper
  • Plagiarism: Should not exceed 15% (Turnitin/iThenticate)
  • IOS Press Author Instructions – FAIA/ATDE Series
  • Templates will be made available on the official website.

Publication and Indexing

  • All accepted and presented papers will be published in the IOS Press FAIA/ATDE series, which is indexed in Scopus, DBLP, and Google Scholar.
  • Proceedings will be available in the IOS Press Digital Library.
  • Selected extended papers will be recommended for SCI/SCIE/Scopus journals, subject to review.
