Registration

All participants whose papers are accepted for presentation and publication must register for the conference. Registration is also open to attendees, students, industry delegates, and academicians who wish to attend sessions, keynotes, and workshops without presenting a paper. Registration includes access to:

  • Keynote and technical sessions
  • Conference kit and ID badge
  • Coffee breaks and lunch during conference days
  • Certificate of participation/presentation

Note: At least one author must register for each accepted paper for it to be included in the final proceedings. Each registration covers only one paper. For additional papers by the same author, an extra fee applies.
