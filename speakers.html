Speakers

Meet the esteemed speakers of ICASAIB-2025

Indian Speakers

Dr. R. Balasubramanian
IIT Madras, INDIA





A senior professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering, Dr. Balasubramanian specializes in biomedical signal processing and AI-based health diagnostics. His research involves developing smart wearable systems for monitoring neurological and cardiac conditions.

Dr. Shwetak Patel
Director of Health Technologies, Google Health




A pioneer in mobile health sensing, Dr. Patel develops AI-integrated smart devices using everyday electronics (like smartphones) for bio-signal monitoring. His innovations enable non-invasive, accessible healthcare diagnostics using AI.

Dr. Debatosh Guha
University of Calcutta, INDIA





Dr. Guha is a specialist in smart antennas and RF systems with applications in biomedical telemetry. His research includes wearable sensor networks and non-contact bio-signal acquisition using smart electronics.

Dr. Ritu Bajaj
General Manager, BIRAC, Department of Biotechnology, India





Dr. Bajaj promotes industrial R&D and startup innovation in AI-powered bioengineering. She facilitates funding and industry-academia collaborations for developing smart healthcare devices and AI-enabled diagnostic tools.

International Speakers

Prof. Ali Khademhosseini
TIBI, USA/UAE





An innovator in smart biomaterials and AI in tissue engineering, Prof. Khademhosseini leads breakthroughs in intelligent drug delivery systems and personalized regenerative medicine using AI for medical decision-making.

Prof. Gert Cauwenberghs
UC San Diego, USA





He specializes in neuroengineering and adaptive bioelectronics, with a focus on real-time brain-computer interfaces and smart bio-signal processors using AI. His work supports neural rehabilitation and assistive devices.

Dr. Dina Katabi





Dr. Katabi’s work focuses on wireless smart sensors and AI-powered ambient intelligence for non-contact health tracking (e.g., respiration, sleep, mobility). Her innovations are used in real-world clinical settings, blending AI and electronics seamlessly
