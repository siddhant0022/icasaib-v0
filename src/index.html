ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

TThe conference focuses on the seamless integration of smart electronics, artificial intelligence, and computational intelligence to revolutionize healthcare technologies. Its strengths lie in providing a multidisciplinary platform that bridges cutting-edge research and real-world applications, fostering collaboration among academia, industry, and healthcare professionals. Unlike similar events, ICECNH-2026 emphasizes the convergence of hardware innovations with intelligent algorithms tailored specifically for next-generation, patient-centric healthcare solutions.

The conference offers unique opportunities to explore emerging technologies such as wearable devices, medical IoT, AI-driven diagnostics, and healthcare robotics under one roof. This comprehensive approach ensures the event’s added value as a catalyst for innovation, knowledge exchange, and impactful partnerships in smart healthcare development.
